The Div-element is the most generic Block level element

The Span-element is the super generic Inline element



Example:

*** DIV 1: Some content here ***
*** DIV 2: Following right after div 1 ***
*** SPAN 1: Following right after div 2 *** *** SPAN 2: Following right after span 1 ***
*** DIV 3: Following right after span 2 *** SPAN 3: INSIDE div 3 *** Continue content of div 3 ***


Warning: Should not use span inside the div element

<span> *** SPAN 4: Some content here *** <div > DIV 4: Following right after span 4 *** </div> </span>
